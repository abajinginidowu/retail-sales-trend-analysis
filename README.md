# Retail Sales Trend and Promotion Impact Analysis
# Project Overview

This project analyzes daily retail sales data from 10 stores between January 2022 and December 2023. The goal is to understand how store performance, promotions, holidays, and seasonal patterns influence retail sales and to generate insights that support better business decisions.

# Business Problem

Retail businesses need to understand the factors that influence sales performance across different stores and time periods. Management wants to know whether promotional campaigns and holiday periods increase revenue, and how sales vary across seasons. By analyzing historical sales data, businesses can identify patterns that support better marketing decisions, inventory planning, and operational strategies.

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains simulated daily sales records for 10 retail stores over a two year period from 2022 to 2023.

Columns included in the dataset:

Date: The date of the sales record

store: Store ID ranging from 1 to 10

sales: Total sales amount recorded for that day in Indian Rupees

Promo: Indicates whether a promotion was active.

1 means promotion active

0 means no promotion

Holiday: Indicates whether the day was a holiday.

1 means holiday

0 means normal day

# Data Preparation

The dataset was imported into Excel and converted into a structured table. Helper columns were created to support time based analysis including year, month, and day columns. These additional columns helped identify seasonal sales patterns and trends across the two year period.

# Key Questions

Which store generates the highest and lowest sales?

How do sales change across different months of the year?

Do promotions increase average daily sales?

Do holidays influence customer spending?

Which time periods show the strongest retail demand?

# Analysis Performed

Store performance analysis

Total sales were analyzed across the 10 stores to identify which stores generated the most and least revenue.

Seasonal sales trend analysis

Monthly sales trends were examined to understand how sales change throughout the year and to identify peak and low demand periods.

Promotion impact analysis

Sales performance during promotional periods was compared with non promotional periods to determine whether promotions increase sales.

Holiday sales analysis

Average sales on holidays were compared with normal days to evaluate whether holidays influence customer spending behavior.

# Key Findings

Store 9 generated the highest sales across the two year period, while store 10 recorded the lowest sales.

Sales followed a seasonal pattern. December recorded the highest sales while February had the lowest sales before increasing rapidly in March.

Promotional activities had a positive impact on sales. Average sales were higher when promotions were active.

Holiday periods also produced higher average sales compared to normal days.

# Conclusion

Retail sales across the 10 stores are influenced by seasonal demand, promotional campaigns, and holiday periods. Sales performance also varies across store locations, indicating that some stores perform more strongly than others.

# Recommendations

Increase promotional activities during low sales months such as February to stimulate demand.

Prepare for peak sales periods such as December by increasing inventory and staffing.

Study the strategies used by the highest performing store and apply similar approaches to improve performance across other locations.

Investigate the causes of lower performance in store 10 and adjust marketing or operational strategies to improve results.
